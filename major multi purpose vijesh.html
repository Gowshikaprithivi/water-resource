Major Multi Prupose Projects in India

  • What is Multi purpose project ?

    water resource projects are planned for variuos purpose like irrigation ,hydro power generation ,water supplied for drinking and industrial purpose , flood conrol ,navigation etc projects which serves than one more purpose are called Multi purpose project

Beas Project: It is a joint venture of the government of punjab , Haryana and Rajasthan. It consists of two units; 1.Beas-Sutlej Link 2.Beas-dam at Pong.

The project links the Beas and sutlej rivers in punjab through 38.4km of hills and valleys.

The waters of the Beas were poured into the mighty Sutlej river on july 10 1977 at the first ever man made conflunce of the two major rivers at slapper in himachal in a mighty bid to augment the water resources of the Gobind Sagar Lake of Bhakra complex.

Bhakra Nangel: Project (Himachal Pradhesh) Largest Multi purpose project in india and the highest straight gravity dam in the world (225.5m high) on the river sutlej.

Sankosh Hydel-Power Project: India and Bhutan have signed an agreement for building of a gigantic Sankosh hydel power project

It will be one among the ten largest projets in Asia

The project is to be constructed near kerabari in Gaylegphug district of Bhutan on Sankosh River.

It is estimated price is Rs 2000 cr

This project will generate 1,525 Mw of power and help irrigate eight lakh hectares of Land
